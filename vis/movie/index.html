We're In The Money!

About

How much does a good movie make? How about a bad one?

This visualization lets you explore the connections between movie ratings, budgets, and profits.

The x-axis shows the film's profit margin while the y-axis gives the Rotten Tomatoes review percentage (the % of critics that give a positive review to the movie). Each dot in the visualization represents a movie. Hover over the dot to get the specifics on that film.
